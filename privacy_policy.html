Privacy Policy for Twitter Info Hider

Last updated: [Current Date]

Overview

Twitter Info Hider is a Chrome extension that helps you focus on content by hiding certain information on Twitter/X. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

Our extension:

  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not track your browsing history
  • Does not send any data to external servers
  • Only stores your preferences locally in your browser

Data Storage

The extension uses Chrome's storage API to save your preferences locally. This includes:

  • Your hiding preferences (what elements to hide)
  • Blur intensity settings
  • Keyboard shortcut preferences

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services or analytics tools.
